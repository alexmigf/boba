Yellow Message, but wait...

You can also change the text's color if the default white isn't doing it for you. Just add text-{COLOR} to the tag holding the text. This works both for the header text and the body text. You can also add hoverability by adding text-hover-{COLOR}. Check it out! P.S. lighten works too (lighten-text-hover-{#})

Getting Funky

You can also completely change the design of the message component. Only keep the message, message-header and message-body classes and add bg-{COLOR}, text-{COLOR}, or border-{COLOR} to style each component of the message box!

Funky on Hover?

Of course! We just go back to our roots and add bg-{COLOR}-hover or text-{COLOR}-hover.
