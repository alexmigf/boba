Introduction

tl;dr: a lightweight, modular and CSS only framework

Our Story

Boba [boh-buh] (also known as bubble tea, boba tea, pearl milk tea, etc.) originates from the distant land of Taiwan. However, our story begins one spring night at 2am in San Francisco. We started discussing the bulky open source frameworks available and decided we deserve better. That's when boba was born, our own light, modular and CSS only framework. Oh, the name? We're just huge boba fanatics. Like really, the saying an apple a day keeps the doctor away was us, except with boba.

Quick Start

Here's a template to help you get started
<!DOCTYPE html>
<html>
  <head>
    <meta charset="utf-8">
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1">
    <title>Build with Boba!</title>
    <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/COMINGSOON">
  </head>
  <body>
    <section class="section">
      <div class="container">
        <div class="row">
          <div class="col">
            <h1 class="header">
              Hello World
            </h1>
            <h3 class="subheader thin">
              boba is the best!
            </h3>
          </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </section>
  </body>
</html>
